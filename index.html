About me

Hello! I am Camilla De Morais, a Brazilian/Canadian User Experience Designer based in Toronto. When I am not designing, you are likely to find me at the gym. Travel makes up my favourite thing to do in life. I value tangible experiences such as trying new foods and learning about different cultures while meeting new people.

Design allows me to visually express myself by providing solutions to human problems that are not only aesthetically pleasing but user-friendly.

I hold a Criminology degree from the University of Toronto. This, paired with several years of customer service experience, seta me apart as an analytical and critical thinker, who enjoys analyzing human behaviour to develop simple solutions to user problems.

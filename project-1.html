Binge Buddies

Imagine watching a TV show or movie with friends and sharing reactions, each person in the comfort of their own home? Binge Buddies allows multiple people to simultaneously tune in and watch, comment, react in virtual living rooms.

Research

A total of 8 people were interviewed in order to help us answer the crucial question of what would encourage users to use a virtual watching experience. We also used survey monkey to provide a quantitative element to our research. Our findings led us to find our who our ideal user would be.

Definition

We organized our user insights into an affitinity, which later led us to developing our user persona and ideal user - Neil Morgan. By grouping together similar pain points, indulgences, biases and preferences, we further developed an empathy map which helped us ideate an ideal solution.

Ideation

Our primary ideation methods consisted of: Storyboarding, Feature Prioritization Matrix and Journey Mapping. Based on the findings each designer sketched their own version of the app as they envisioned the solution. We iterated each sketch and combined the similarities and the strongest aspects of each version. We further tested these sketches and iterated prior to creating lo-fi digital wireframes.

Prototyping

We used the Whimsical tool to develop our task and user flows which we later translated into lo-fidelity digital, clickable wireframes. We conducted 5 usability tests which we used to iterate our design prior to creating a final, hi-fidelity wireframes.

Testing

Our hi-fidelity wireframe was tested using the guerrilla testing method. We wanted to ensure our final design was intuitive and user friendly. Based on feedback from our users, we iterated our final design to ensure use of the app was seamless and each task was completed with fewest number of clicks.

Conclusion

In conclusion our app was able to demonstrate the end-to-end virtual collective move/show watching experience. The case study concludes with opportunities for monetizing the app such as paid ‘top shelf’ section and an active exclusive chat and review section.
